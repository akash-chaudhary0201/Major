About Us

Who are we?

KITABI SEHER is an ecommerce platform that specializes in selling second hand books to underprivileged children. Our mission is to make books accessible to every child regardless of their socio-economic background.
We believe that every child has the right to education and that books are an essential tool for learning and personal growth.

What is Our Mission And Vision?

Our vision is to create a world where every child has access to the knowledge and inspiration found in books. We strive to be a platform that provides a wide range of affordable, high-quality second-hand books for children of all ages.
We work closely with our partners, volunteers, and donors to ensure that our books are in good condition and are appropriate for our target audience.
